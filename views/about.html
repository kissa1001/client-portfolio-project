What I'm About

A freelance writer, editor, promoter, and instructor by day (and non-profit manager by night) , I thrive on variety at work. Whether I'm collaborating with a student or client on persuasive content angles or holed up in my office researching citation style, I love learning more than anything. I'm always on the hunt for new opportunities in work and project collaboration, so please don't hesitate to contact me about how I could help your concept grow!

I offer assistance in a variety of writing, editing, and promotional tasks, and I'm available for consulting on communications-related topics as well.
