About Me

A freelance writer, editor, promoter, and seven-year college instructor by day (non-profit manager by night), I thrive on variety at work. Whether I'm collaborating with a client on persuasive content angles or researching citation styles with students, I love learning more than anything.

I'm always on the hunt for new opportunities in work and project collaboration, so please don't hesitate to contact me about how I could help your concept grow! I offer assistance in a variety of writing, editing, and promotional tasks, as well as consulting on communications-related topics.
